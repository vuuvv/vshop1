Signing in

If you launch Maqetta from a remote server, the first time you launch Maqetta, you will need to log in to the environment. The login screen is usually provided by the hosting site. After logging in, the hosting site will take you into the application.

(Note: if you install Maqetta locally and therefore run Maqetta at http://localhost:50000, you do not need to go through the login process.)

After signing in, you will see the Maqetta Workspace, containing a Welcome page in the center and palettes to the left and right sides. The palettes are empty until you open a file, except for the Files palette on the lower left area of the workspace.
